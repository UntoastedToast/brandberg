Geographic Information System

About

This is a Geographic Information System of the Brandberg. The presentation focuses especially on the found paintings and their number of figures. These can be put into context by means of the GIS, for example by displaying topographical conditions or the distance to possible stretch of water.

Website created by Janik Lierfeld. Images and text were taken from a Wikipedia article.
